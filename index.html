Home Privacy Policy Terms of Service

ClipBot is a personal, non-commercial automation tool built by an individual developer. It surfaces popular, publicly available clips from Twitch, converts them to a short vertical format, and publishes them as YouTube Shorts on a single dedicated channel operated by the developer: youtube.com/@TwitchClips_xi.

Every video credits the original streamer by name with a link back to their Twitch channel. Streamers who would prefer their clips not be reposted can request removal at any time — see the contact details in the Privacy Policy, and takedown requests are honored the same day.
